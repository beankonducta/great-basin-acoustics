About Me

I'm Andrew Goldring, and I specialize in transforming dining spaces into acoustically balanced environments where conversation flows as smoothly as the service. Drawing from my years of experience in professional music production, I bring a uniquely refined approach to restaurant acoustics, understanding that the perfect dining atmosphere requires careful attention to both sound control and ambient energy.

My tailored acoustic solutions address the specific challenges faced by restaurants, bars, and cafes, where hard surfaces and open designs often create unwanted noise reflection. By implementing strategic sound treatment through custom-designed absorption panels, diffusers, and targeted acoustic modifications, I help establishments maintain their aesthetic appeal while creating an intimate atmosphere where patrons can comfortably converse without competing with excessive ambient noise or experiencing sound fatigue.
